Father Agnel School, Noida

Our School

Fr. Agnel School Noida is a Roman Catholic school situated in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. The school is run by the Agnel Charities of the Society of the Missionaries of St. Francis Xavier, of Catholic Priests and Brothers. Provides education from Pre Nursery to 12th. Father Agnel School, Noida was established in the year 2001 with a view to provide excellent education as per the principles of equality, love and brotherhood. The school has been accorded affiliation with the C.B.S.E. The school has boarding facilities for 250 students and is equipped with excellent facilities, a large football/cricket ground, swimming pool and open - air as well as enclosed auditoria. The school also has day boarding facilities.
